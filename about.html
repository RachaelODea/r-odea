Global Approach

Unlike traditional agencies, R-ODEA also works alongside mother agents, acting as an extension of the team, offering market entry expertise, PR campaigns, and partnership brokering that respect existing representation while unlocking new opportunities across China, Hong Kong, and the wider Asian region.

R-ODEA is an independent consultancy partnering with brands and talent across fashion, art, travel, and beauty. Innovative, globally fluent, and results-driven, we align business ambition with cultural energy to deliver elevated commercial success.

With hubs in London, Hong Kong, and Melbourne, we activate across press, events, VIP engagement, brand partnerships, and strategic communications.

Founded on the belief that the industry is evolving, we craft defining cultural moments that connect brands to new audiences and support a global community of tastemakers and creatives expanding into Asia and beyond.

Breaking from the traditional agency model, R-ODEA partners directly with talent and brands while also collaborating alongside mother agents as an extension of their team, offering market-entry expertise, PR campaigns, and partnership brokering that unlock new opportunities across China, Hong Kong, and the wider Asian region.
